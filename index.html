SUPERSTORE

The Superstore dataset contains data on order details of customers for orders of a superstore in the US.

This includes the state, region, order date, shipping date, product ordered etc.

For the perfromance analysis ,we have considered data from 2014 to 2016

The Performance Analysis has 3 charts

1.Profit over years 2.Profit by States 3.Profit distribution among sub-cateogries of a category
